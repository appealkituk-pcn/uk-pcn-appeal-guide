How to Appeal a Private Parking Charge in the UK

If you have received a private parking charge notice in the UK, you are not automatically required to pay it. Many notices can be successfully appealed.

Step-by-step appeal process

  1. Check whether the notice complies with the Protection of Freedoms Act 2012.
  2. Review the signage at the car park for clarity and visibility.
  3. Confirm that the vehicle details are accurate.
  4. Submit an initial appeal to the parking operator.
  5. If rejected, escalate the appeal to POPLA or the IAS where applicable.

Using the correct wording is critical when submitting an appeal.
